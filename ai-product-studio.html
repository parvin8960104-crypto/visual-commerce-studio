FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is AI-generated product photography different from traditional photography? ▾

AI product photography uses machine learning models to generate photorealistic images from your product files, brand guidelines, and creative briefs. It achieves identical or superior visual quality to traditional photography, with no physical studio, no models, no travel — and at a fraction of the cost and time.

How long does a typical project take? ▾

Standard projects are delivered within 48–72 hours. Complex campaigns or large volume orders may take 5–7 business days. Rush delivery (12–24 hours) is available for urgent projects.

Do you work with international brands? ▾

Absolutely. We work with brands globally and have delivered projects across the US, Europe, Middle East, and Asia. Communication is in English, Persian, and Arabic.

What do you need from me to get started? ▾

To begin, we need: (1) Product images or 3D files, (2) Brand guidelines if available, (3) Usage context (Amazon, Instagram, print ad, etc.), (4) Reference images for style direction.

Are the final images licensed for commercial use? ▾

Yes. All final deliverables are fully licensed for commercial use with unlimited usage rights — print, digital, broadcast, and social media. You own the files completely.
